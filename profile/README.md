## Who we are

Flowzify is a software and AI automation studio based in Kharadi, Pune. We work with founders and small teams who need something *built* — not a deck, not a roadmap, a working product — and we build it fast, then keep iterating alongside them.

## What we do

| | |
|---|---|
| 🛠️&nbsp; **Custom software** | Full-stack web and mobile builds scoped to what a founder actually needs, not a template. |
| 🤖&nbsp; **AI integrations** | LLM-powered features and internal automations that slot into workflows a team already has. |
| 📱&nbsp; **Cross-platform apps** | One codebase, shipped to iOS, Android, and web. |

## Products

**OfficeIQ**
A custom GPT on the ChatGPT Store built for workplace communication — drafting messages, checking tone, and navigating the everyday back-and-forth of office life.

**Wrkdock** *(formerly Solo Studio)*
A business OS for freelancers and solo operators. Clients, projects, proposals, invoices, and income tracking — one dashboard instead of five tabs.

More tools are in the pipeline, most carrying the **IQ** name where it fits.

## How we build

We come out of a hackathon background, and it shows in the process: short cycles, working demos over long specs, and a bias toward shipping something real this week instead of a perfect plan next quarter.
