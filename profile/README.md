## Who we are

Flowzify is a software and AI automation studio in Kharadi, Pune. We build for founders and small teams — custom software, AI integrations, mobile apps — and we move fast.

## What we do

- Custom software — full-stack web and mobile, scoped to the actual problem
- AI integrations — LLM features and automations wired into workflows teams already use
- Cross-platform apps — one codebase, iOS, Android, and web

## Products

**OfficeIQ** — a custom GPT on the ChatGPT Store for workplace communication: drafting, tone-checking, day-to-day office messaging.

**Solo Studio** — a business OS for freelancers: clients, projects, proposals, invoices, and income tracking.

More IQ-branded tools are in development.

## How we build

Hackathon-honed process — short cycles, working demos, ship this week rather than plan next quarter.
